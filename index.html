Hiking in Massachusetts

Discover Trails and Adventures

Massachusetts has some of the most beautiful hiking trails in New England. From mountain summits to seaside walks, there’s a trail for everyone.

Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced hiker, you’ll find places that you will enjoy.

Top Reasons to Hike in Massachusetts

  • Accessible trails for all skill levels
  • Lots of history and landmarks
